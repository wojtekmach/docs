    Req.Conn.Headers (req v0.6.0-dev)

    View Source

    Opaque container for Req request/response headers and trailers.

    Construct with one of req_headers/1, resp_headers/1, req_trailers/1, or resp_trailers/1. Each kind knows which header atoms are valid for its side.

    Implements Enumerable and Access. Lookups are case-insensitive. headers["x"] returns the first matching value.

    Names may be passed as strings (taken as-is, downcased) or as atoms. Atoms must be in well_known_req_headers/0 (for :req_* kinds) or well_known_resp_headers/0 (for :resp_* kinds). Unknown atoms raise ArgumentError.
